Title: Picture Poker

High Concept

  • Remake of the minigame Picture Poker from Super Mario 64 DS
  • Plays like Five Card Draw, it's a simple variant of Poker

Genre

Genres: Card Game, Strategy

Platforms

Windows Only!

Story

The story is purely abstract.

Aesthetics

Graphics: Cartoonish

Sounds: 8-bit, looped

Sound will play for selecting card, and there will be background music

Gameplay

Mechanics

Gambling, Luck

The player has no real choice in the outcome, they just have to hope to get a good hand

Controls

The game is purely controlled by Mouse

Onboarding

There will be a guide of playable hand types to the side of the website. Cards will be obviously selectable and the buttons to draw and play will be clearly labeled. Might include a small How To Play section

Mockups

Other

The concept of this game changed so many times. Every idea I had before this one had some fault to it. I eventually came up with trying to recreate the Wanted! minigame from Super Mario 64 DS as it had been trending recently. I tried figuring out how the specifics would work, but decided it would be too much work and switched gears to another minigame from the same game. I landed on Picture Poker, which was a game I quite enjoyed, as I had made a Poker game in Java a few years ago. Ended up being a lot more work than I had thought. If the fact that is too similar to the game it is based off of is an issue, I can pivot and change the graphics and some of the core gameplay. The depth in this game comes less from the gameplay itself, and more of the coding it takes to make it. Looking into potentionally implementing some things from JQuery but need to research JQuery more.
